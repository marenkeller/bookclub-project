Book of the month

The Secret History

by Donna Tartt

Our first book for the fall season is the one and only modern classic The Secret History by Donna Tartt. Published in 1992, the novel celebrates its 30th anniversary this year. Set in a frictional New England liberal arts college, Tartt writes the story that has fueled the so-called dark academia subculture.
