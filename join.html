Welcome!

The Seasonal Reader is an online book club with books curated for each season. We send out 12 books a year perfectly suited to the time of year, from contemporary literature to classic fiction, from translated short stories to memoirs and non-fiction. All books are in English. Book boxes will be dispatched four times a year, at the beginning of every season. Your subscription runs until you cancel.
