The Seasonal Reader

About Us

The Seasonal Reader is an online book club with books curated for each season.
Have you ever noticed that as the weather changes you want to read something cozy? Or funny? Or something that gives a certain vibe?
We have.
Our philosophy is that the reading experience can be improved by reading books that in different ways compliment the time of year.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you ship worldwide?
Yes, we do.

When can I expect my books?
We dispatch bundles of the three titles at the beginning of every season. You can expect a package early in the following months: December, March, June and September.

Who is the Book Club for?
The Seasonal Reader is for everyone who loves to read. We aim to select books that will enrapture, inspire, bring joy and create discussion among all our subscribers. That is why our reading list is carefully selected not just to fit the seaosns, but also to be inclusive and intersectional.

Can I Give a Subcription as a gift?

Are there any hidden costs?
Shipping is not included in the prices, but will be calculated at the check-out.
